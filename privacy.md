Your privacy matters to us. This page explains what information is (and isn't)
collected when you visit Cozy Life Picks.

## What we collect

Cozy Life Picks is a static website. We do not require accounts, and we do not
collect personal information from visitors beyond what's described below.

- **If you sign up for our email newsletter**, we collect the email address
  you provide, using Mailchimp to send occasional updates. You can unsubscribe
  at any time using the link in any email we send.
- **Site analytics.** We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use
  the site (pages viewed, links clicked) so we can improve it. This does not
  identify you personally.

## Third parties

- **Hosting.** This site is served by our hosting provider, which may log
  standard technical data (such as IP addresses) to operate the service.
- **Fonts.** We load fonts from Google Fonts, which may receive standard
  request data from your browser.
- **Analytics.** Google Analytics may set cookies to measure site usage. See
  [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details.
- **Email.** If you subscribe to our newsletter, Mailchimp processes and
  stores your email address on our behalf. See
  [Mailchimp's Privacy Policy](https://www.intuit.com/privacy/statement/).
- **Affiliate links.** When you click a link to a retailer such as Amazon,
  that site's own privacy policy and cookies apply from that point on.

## Questions

Email us at **cozylifepicks@gmail.com** and we'll be happy to help.
