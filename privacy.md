Your privacy matters to us. This page explains what information is (and isn't)
collected when you visit Cozy Life Picks.

## What we collect

Cozy Life Picks is a static website. We do not require accounts, we do not set
tracking cookies ourselves, and we do not collect personal information from
visitors.

## Third parties

- **Hosting.** This site is served by our hosting provider, which may log
  standard technical data (such as IP addresses) to operate the service.
- **Fonts.** We load fonts from Google Fonts, which may receive standard
  request data from your browser.
- **Affiliate links.** When you click a link to a retailer such as Amazon,
  that site's own privacy policy and cookies apply from that point on.
